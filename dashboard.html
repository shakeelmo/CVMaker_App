Import from LinkedIn PDF

Download your LinkedIn profile as a PDF, then upload it here.

  1. Click the Me icon at the top of your LinkedIn homepage.
  2. Click View profile.
  3. If you wish to save a LinkedIn member’s profile as a PDF file, navigate to their profile.
  4. Click the More or Resources button in the introduction section.
  5. Select Save to PDF from the dropdown.

Upload that PDF here and we will extract it into your resume editor.
